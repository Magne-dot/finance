HOW IT WORKS

Data pipeline: When you analyze a stock, the app fetches fundamentals from Finnhub and FMP, price history from Yahoo Finance, and recent news. This structured data is sent to Claude, which synthesizes it into a scored report.

Scoring model: Each stock gets 0–100 across six factors: Quality (25%), Valuation (20%), Momentum (15%), Catalysts (20%), Sentiment (10%), Dividends (10%). The composite score drives the overall verdict.

Catalyst detection: Claude uses web search to find upcoming earnings dates, product launches, regulatory decisions, macro events — anything that could move the stock price in the next 3–6 months.
